Architecture

Not everyone knows this, but the Sheffield's Student Union consists of three buildings.

The original was the Graves Building, built in the 1930's. The building was deisigned by Stephen Welsh and was later donated to the students by a local buisnessman and city benefactor, J. G. Graves.

The University of Sheffield then built on two more buildings from 1959-1962. These were the University House and the Link Building.

The second two buildings have been extended multiple times since 1994.

One of the most impressive additions was the new main entrance, which was finished in 2010.

Mathers, H.,(2007). Standing up for students. Sheffield: The University of Sheffield Union of Students.
