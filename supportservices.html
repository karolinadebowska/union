Support Services

Student Advice Centre

The Student Advice Centre can aid students in a variety of ways. They can provide invaluable advice and counseling for students in need of support. The advisers are accessible through appointments with a wide range of available times to suit all schedules. The University website asks students to book an appointment by visiting the Student Advice Centre Reception Desk or by calling 0114 222 8660. According to the University's website, the appointments are 20 minutes long. If you can not come speak to someone in person, you can email advice@sheffield.ac.uk, and they aim to reply within 5 working days. The Student Advice Centre is located on the 3rd level of the Students' Union, near the southern exit.

Student Job Shop

If you are struggling to find a job and in need of help, visit the Student Job Shop! The service connects students with a wide range of job offers. From part-time paid work and summer internships to vacation work and volunteer opportunities, the Job Shop has it all. The center is located on the 3rd level of the Students' Union, near the Student Services Information Desk.

Financial Support Team

The financial support team can help connect students with scholarship opportunities and extra funds set aside for students in need. If you are facing "legitimate, higher than average financial demands", as determined by the University's website statement, then you might have access to extra funds to help you through your education.

International Student Support

If you're an international student, you might face a variety of complications while studying at The University of Sheffield. That is why the Students' Union provides specialized advice for international students. If you have any Visa or immigration concerns, ask the team to help! Having trouble settling in or facing cultural shock? Get in touch with the events set in place to help international students connect. Any questions about language requirements or bringing a family dependant with you can be answered by the International Student Support team!
