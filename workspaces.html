Working Spaces

The Students' Union provides various working spaces throughout the building. From gallery rooms to a meeting space with a beautiful garden view, the Union accommodates for all working occasions. The Sheffield's Union is a perfect place to work with a team, have organised meetings, or simply study. To book a room, go to the Students' Union Room Bookings system.
